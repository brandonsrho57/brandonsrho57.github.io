10/10!

How can I not? Minecraft has not only set the standard for modern open-world games, but has completely changed how we as gamers see it. Minecraft has offered its audience various ways to enjoy the game, whether casually or 'hardcore'.


Before Minecraft, the only games that had stocked in the gaming shelves of your local Walmart were The Sims or World of Warcraft. With the introduction of Minecraft, people of all ages and interests came to enjoy the gameplay and simple design.


With the recent updates, Minecraft is constantly evolving into a game with unlimited intricacies.
